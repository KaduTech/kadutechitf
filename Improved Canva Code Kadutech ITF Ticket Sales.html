About Ikorodu Tech Fair 2026

Join us for the most anticipated technology event in Lagos State, where innovation meets opportunity and the future of African tech takes center stage.

Empowering Tech Innovation in Ikorodu

The Ikorodu Tech Fair 2026 is more than just a conference – it's a catalyst for technological transformation in our community. We bring together industry leaders, emerging startups, students, and tech enthusiasts to share knowledge, forge partnerships, and drive innovation forward.

Our mission is to position Ikorodu as a leading tech hub in Lagos State while providing a platform for local talent to connect with global opportunities. Experience cutting-edge technology demonstrations, insightful workshops, and networking opportunities that will shape your tech journey.

Featured Speaker

Learn from industry leaders shaping the future of technology

Damilola Ogunfile
CEO, KaduTech Nigeria

Keynote: "Building Scalable Tech Solutions for Africa"

Join Damilola Ogunfile, the visionary CEO of KaduTech Nigeria, as he shares insights on building technology solutions that scale across African markets. With over a decade of experience in the Nigerian tech ecosystem, Damilola will discuss the challenges and opportunities in creating sustainable tech businesses that serve local communities while competing globally.

Key Topics:

  • Scaling tech startups in emerging markets
  • Building products for African consumers
  • Funding strategies for African tech companies
  • The future of fintech in Nigeria
